BlackJack: The Game

Let's play

  • Your goal is to score the highest points possible within 1-21.
  • Each numbered card 2-10 adds point equivalent to their points.
  • King, Queen, Jack = 10 points each.
  • Ace can be both 1 or 11. if your point is less than 10 it will add 11 points. If not it will add 1 point.
